What This Page Is

  • A free public bulletin board that connects people needing off-road recovery with volunteers
  • No professional help is offered. Skill and experience vary by volunteer
  • Both the recoveree and the recoverer act at their own risk

Safety and Legal Basics

  • Practice safe operations during any recovery
  • No operating or recovering a vehicle while under the influence
  • Obey property laws. Get permission to access non-public areas
  • Only recover a vehicle with the owner present and consenting
  • Take clear before and after photos
  • This is a free service. No compensation may be demanded for standard recoveries

Nothing on this site is professional instruction.

Posting Guidance for Requests

  • Share multiple photos of the vehicle and surroundings
  • Drop a pin and include access directions and landmarks
  • State permission to access the area if relevant
  • List preferred or allowed recovery methods and gear if you have a preference
  • When finished, edit your post title to add RECOVERED
