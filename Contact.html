What This Page Is

  • A free, public bulletin board that helps connect people needing off-road recovery with volunteers.
  • No professional help is offered. Skill and experience vary by volunteer.
  • Both the recoveree and the recoverer act at their own risk.

This site aims to serve the entire United States. Local groups may have their own rules and moderators.

Safety and Legal Basics

  • Practice safe operations during any recovery. You are responsible for your own actions and equipment.
  • No operating or recovering a vehicle while under the influence. If suspected, do not engage.
  • Obey property laws. Get permission to access non-public areas. No trespassing.
  • Only recover a vehicle with the owner present and consenting.
  • Take clear before and after photos of the vehicle and recovery area.
  • This is a free service. No compensation may be demanded for standard recoveries.
  • Wrecker or paid services must clearly state fees up front and may be refused by the requester.

Nothing on this site is professional instruction. Use your best judgment and comply with all laws.

Posting Guidance for Requests

  • Share multiple photos of the stuck vehicle and surroundings.
  • If no exact address, drop a pin and include access directions and landmarks.
  • State permission to access the area if relevant.
  • List preferred or allowed recovery methods and gear if you have a preference.
  • When you are out, edit your post title to add RECOVERED and leave the rest for history.

Status updates and coordination should happen on the original post, not through third-party dispatch tools.

Moderation and Conduct

  • Keep posts on topic for recovery requests. Use the chat group for general discussion.
  • No derogatory, racial, sexual, or slanderous content. Such posts will be removed.
  • Advertising and solicitations are not allowed except official merchandise or as otherwise noted by admins.
  • Admins may remove posts or members at their discretion for violations or disruptive behavior.
