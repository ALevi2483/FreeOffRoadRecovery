Free Off-Road Recovery

Get Unstuck With Community Help

We connect drivers with volunteers for no-cost off-road recovery. Public bulletin board. No professional services offered.

🚙 How It Works

Post your situation with photos and a pinned location, or use our directory to locate nearby volunteers. Coordination happens directly between you and volunteers. There is no dispatch center and response times vary.

Search by state and city or view the volunteer map on the Find page.

🧭 Safety First

Everyone participates at their own risk. Do not operate or recover a vehicle while under the influence. Get permission for private property. Take before and after photos. If a situation is dangerous or life threatening, call 911.

Read the FAQ and Terms before you post.

🤝 Become a Volunteer

Have recovery gear and want to help nearby drivers? Apply to be listed. Use a public landmark for your pin if you prefer not to share your home address.
