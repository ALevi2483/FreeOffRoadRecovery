How It Works

1.Join

Complete the volunteer form to be added to the map/directory.

2.Connect

Drivers contact you directly with a GPS pin and situation details.

3.Recover

Attempt safe recoveries within your capability. Tips for gas are optional.

Volunteer Requirements

  • Basic off-road recovery knowledge and good judgment.
  • Appropriate recovery points on your vehicle.
  • Bring basic gear (see recommended list below).
  • Follow local laws, land-use rules, and respect private property.
  • No charging fees. Voluntary tips/gas money allowed.

Recommended Gear

Recovery Basics
  • Rated recovery/tow strap
  • Soft shackles or rated bow shackles
  • Front & rear recovery points
Traction & Tools
  • Traction boards & shovel
  • Tire deflator & air compressor
  • Gloves, lights, first aid

A winch is helpful but not required. Attempt only what you can perform safely.

Expectations

  • Communicate clearly about risks, access, and meet-up location.
  • It’s okay to decline a recovery that looks unsafe or beyond your capability.
  • Use proper anchors; avoid tow balls and unsafe attachment points.
  • Keep bystanders clear of straps, lines, and snap-back zones.
