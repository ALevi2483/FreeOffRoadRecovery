How to use

  1. Open the map and find nearby volunteers.
  2. Contact one or more directly.
  3. Share a GPS pin, vehicle details, terrain, and hazards.
  4. Agree on a safe meet up plan.

What to share

  • GPS coordinates or a dropped pin
  • Make and model, recovery points, tire size
  • Terrain and hazards: mud depth, water, snow, slope
  • Gear on hand: straps, boards, winch, shackles
Safety first
  • If anyone is in danger, call 911.
  • Stand clear of straps, lines, and snap back zones.
  • Use proper recovery points. Avoid tow balls.
