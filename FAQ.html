Free Off-Road Recovery Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

This site is a public bulletin board that connects people who need off-road recovery with volunteers. It is not a dispatch center or a business.

How do I request recovery help?

Post in our bulletin board at FACEBOOK_GROUP_NAME with photos, a pinned location, and access directions. You can also use REQUEST_FORM_URL or check the map on the Find page.

How fast will someone respond?

There is no guaranteed response time. Volunteers reply when they can.

Does this cost money?

Standard recoveries are free. Paid tow services must disclose fees and may be refused.

What info should I include?
  • Photos of the vehicle and terrain
  • Pinned GPS location or exact address and access route
  • Vehicle details and hazards
  • Preferred or allowed recovery methods if you have preferences
What if my situation becomes dangerous?

Call 911 first. Volunteers are not a substitute for emergency services.

Who are the volunteers?

Independent off-road enthusiasts with varying experience and gear. They are not screened or supervised by this site.

Who is responsible for damage?

Each participant is responsible for their own vehicle and equipment. Take before and after photos.

Is trespassing allowed?

No. Get permission to access private property and follow all laws.

How do I get listed on the map?

Fill out VOLUNTEER_FORM_URL and provide a geocodable landmark for your pin.

How do I update or remove my listing?

Use the form option to update an existing record or email CONTACT_EMAIL.
