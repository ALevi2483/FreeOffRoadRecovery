FAQ

Answers to common questions about how Free Off-Road Recovery works. Please read before posting or responding to requests.

Is this a professional service?

No. This is a community bulletin board for volunteers. Use at your own risk.

Does it cost anything?

No. Volunteers offer their time freely. You may choose to offer gas money or a thank-you, but it is not required.

What should I include when I post a request?

  • Your GPS location (pin or coordinates)
  • Vehicle details (make, model, tires, recovery points)
  • Terrain description (mud, sand, snow, water, incline, etc.)
  • Any gear you already have (winch, tow straps, shackles, etc.)

What if I need urgent or emergency help?

Call 911 if anyone’s life or safety is at risk. This site is for community volunteer assistance, not emergency response.

Can I become a volunteer?

Yes. Use the volunteer signup form (link on the main site) to share your info and get added to the map/directory.
