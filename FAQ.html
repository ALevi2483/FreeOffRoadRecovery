Free Off-Road Recovery Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

This site is a public bulletin board that helps connect people who need off-road recovery with volunteers. It is not a dispatch center or a business. Volunteers and requesters act at their own risk.

Requesters Volunteers Safety Legal Listings
How do I request recovery help?

Post in our bulletin board FACEBOOK_GROUP_NAME with photos, the pinned location, access directions, and any hazards. You can also submit the request form at REQUEST_FORM_URL or check the volunteer map at map.html when available.

How fast will someone respond?

There is no guaranteed response time. Volunteers respond when they can. Posting clear info and a precise location improves your chances.

Does this cost money?

Standard recoveries are free. No one may demand payment. If a tow or paid service replies, they must state fees up front and you may refuse service.

What info should I include in my post?
  • Photos from multiple angles of the vehicle and terrain
  • Pinned GPS location or exact address plus access route and landmarks
  • Vehicle make, model, tire size, 2WD or 4WD, and if you have recovery points
  • Hazards like water depth, mud, sand, ice, traffic, or private gates
  • Your preferred or allowed methods and gear if you have a preference
Can I request on behalf of someone else?

Yes, but the owner should be present and consent to recovery before any work starts.

What if my situation becomes dangerous or life threatening?

Call 911 first. Volunteers are not a substitute for emergency services, law enforcement, or professional towing in hazardous conditions.

Who are the volunteers?

Independent off-road enthusiasts with varying experience and equipment. They are not screened or supervised by this site.

What gear is commonly used?

Rated recovery points, soft shackles, recovery ropes or straps, traction boards, winches, tree savers, and basic safety gear. Chains are discouraged.

Who is responsible for damage?

Each participant is responsible for their own vehicle and equipment. By participating you accept the risks. Take before and after photos.

Can volunteers operate after drinking or using drugs?

No. If someone appears impaired do not engage in the recovery.

Is trespassing allowed to reach a stuck vehicle?

No. Get permission to access private property. Obey all laws and land use rules.

What is the difference between recovery and towing?

Recovery is extracting a stuck vehicle using off-road techniques. Towing is transporting a vehicle on public roads and may require licensing and fees. Volunteers do recoveries. Tow companies handle paid transport.

Map Listings and Updates

How do I get listed on the volunteer map?

Fill out the volunteer form at VOLUNTEER_FORM_URL. Provide a geocodable address or landmark for your pin. It does not need to be your home address.

How do I update or remove my listing?

Submit the form with the option to update an existing record or email CONTACT_EMAIL with your changes. Include the name, phone, region, and the updated pin location.

Why do you need a specific address for the pin?

The map needs coordinates to calculate distance and show the closest volunteers. You can use a nearby public landmark if you prefer.

Conduct and Moderation

What posts will be removed?

Derogatory, racial, sexual, or slanderous content. Off topic posts on the main page. Repeated disruptive behavior. Advertising outside of approved items.

Can I advertise my business or paid service?

No, unless explicitly allowed by the admins. Requests for paid work should be clearly labeled and may be refused by requesters.

How do bans or removals work?

Admins may remove posts or members at their discretion for violations. If banned, you may message an admin after one week to ask for a vote on reinstatement. Waiting periods may increase after each no vote.

Legal and Privacy

Is this professional guidance?

No. Nothing on this site is professional instruction. Use your best judgment and follow all laws.

Do you share my data?

See our Privacy and Terms. Request posts are public by design. Forms may collect contact info to connect you with volunteers.

Can I donate?

This is a free community effort. If donations or merchandise become available we will list them on the site. No one should demand payment for a standard recovery.
